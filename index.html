Finnish slot machines now require player ID

The new practice has been introduced to help prevent problem gambling and gambling by minors.

As of Tuesday 12.1.2021, slot machine players have to confirm their identity to play with machines. This new policy is part of social
responsibility programme, aiming to prevent gambling and harms it brings within. Players have to use Veikkaus -issued card
(the operator of these slot machines), mobile phone or by using payment card linked to customer service.

Easier age control

The new policy is also for making sure that nobody under 18-years old can play with slot machines. This helps to prevent future
gambling problems and doesn't encourage teenagers to play and gamble. Veikkaus also tells that these precautions
is to expect cutting over 300 million euros from player losses annually.

Difference between HTML & HTML5?

HTML5 is more user friendly than HMTL because of it's ability to store data locally with user's browser, which also eliminates
needs of HTTP cookies. The result is that content can be delivered much faster and safer than before. HMTL aso ensures consistency
across all browsers. Biggest difference between these two is the integration of video and audio into the language's specifications.
