Finnish slot machines now require player ID

The new practice has been introduced to help prevent problem gambling and gambling by minors.

As of Tuesday 12.1.2021, slot machine players have to confirm their identity to play with machines.

This new policy is part of social responsibility programme, aiming to prevent gambling and harms it brings within.

Players have to use Veikkaus -issued card (the operator of these slot machines),

mobile phone or by using payment card linked to customer service.

Easier age control

The new policy is also for making sure that nobody under 18-years old can play with slot machines.

This helps to prevent future gambling problems and doesn't encourage teenagers to play and gamble.

Veikkaus also tells that these precautions is to expect cutting over 300 million euros from player losses annually.
